About Me

Hello! I'm Austin Green, PharmD, MPH, BCPS, AAHIVP, a visionary healthcare professional with a strong commitment to transforming healthcare through technology and innovation.

With a multidisciplinary background in pharmacy, public health, and data science, I bring a unique perspective to tackling systemic challenges in healthcare. I specialize in healthtech innovation, leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning to enhance patient outcomes while ensuring ethical integration into existing systems.

Currently, I am pursuing advanced certifications and education to further solidify my knowledge in healthtech and AI/ML strategy. My long-term vision includes leading a healthtech startup that focuses on improving healthcare accessibility and efficiency for all.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I am an advocate for education and leadership development, having contributed to academic presentations, publications, and mentorship in the field. Explore my CV and projects to learn more about my journey and impact.
